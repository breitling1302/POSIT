Getting Started

To use POSIT you must have an account on a POSIT server and you need to register your phone with that server.

If you don't already have an account, click the New User button. This will let you create an account and register your phone. You can also do this by visiting the server: http://posit.hfoss.org/sandbox.

If you already have a POSIT account, click the Login button to register your phone.

After login you can create a project where your Finds will be stored. Have fun!.
