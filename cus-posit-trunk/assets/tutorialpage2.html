Welcome to POSIT!

POSIT (Portable Open Search and Identification Tool) lets you collect and share 'finds', which can be any item you want to search for and catalog. POSIT timestamps, geostamps and lets you associate photos and text with your finds, which can then be synced with an online server and shared with other POSIT users.

What is a "find"?

A find is anything that a user has located and would like to catalog using POSIT. A find can be any item of interest from a cool beer pub, an invasive species, a supermarket product, or a structure damaged by natural disaster.
